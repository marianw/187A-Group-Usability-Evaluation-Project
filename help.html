{help and documentation}

“Even though it is better if the system can be used without documentation, it may be necessary to provide help and documentation. Any such information should be easy to search, focused on the user's task, list concrete steps to be carried out, and not be too large.”

Lack of support:
There is no site map but the footer does contain the webmaster’s email (how often they check and respond to emails is another story). Certain pages like ULink, the university’s online services, does provide a link to a FAQ-type page for those who have problems logging in.

Overall:
In general, UL’s site does not supply help or a general FAQ section. It would be helpful to add these to the site for users who encounter problems.
