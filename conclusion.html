Conclusion:
After evaluating the University of Louisiana website based on Nielsenâ€™s 10 heuristics, it is evident that there is much room for improvement. This site lacks consistency and organization. The uninteresting design of this website in regard to its layout and focus on trivial objects such as logos and pictures, instantly discourages the user from exploring further. With a framework based on conveying large amounts of information, the user is presented with frustrating visual expectations. This site sacrifices usability and aesthetic design for the purposes of accumulating additional material.


With a few simple changes to the navigation and consistency, the University of Louisana website has much potential. It uses non-technical language that can be understood by majority of users. Furthermore, it does provide error messages although they do not present a solution.


We believe that the University of Louisiana website has many areas that need fixing. With some major changes, the site has the potential to be a fantastic college website.
