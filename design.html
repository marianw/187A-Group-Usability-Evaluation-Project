{aesthetic and minimalistic design}

“Dialogues should not contain information which is irrelevant or rarely needed. Every extra unit of information in a dialogue competes with the relevant units of information and diminishes their relative visibility.”

Massive Amount of Links:

UL’s site is filled with text and links, which lead to more links, and even more links! Pages are not visually appealing but overwhelming. Scarcity of pictures is definitely a contributing factor. Drop boxes as well as other ways to minimize text and links and incorporating more white space would benefit this website tremendously.

Overall:
The site looks out of date and in need of some major changes in design. Not only would this help the user to more efficiently access information but it would also be more aesthetically pleasing.
